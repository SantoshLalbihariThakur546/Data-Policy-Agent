Data Policy Agent Brief
Project Directive

Automated Compliance Enforcement

The corporate landscape is shifting. Compliance requirements and business policies are locked away in unstructured PDF documents, while company databases evolve rapidly. Manual auditing is no longer sustainable.

Your Mission: Build a software-only "Data Policy Agent" that bridges the gap between static policy documents and dynamic company data. The agent must autonomously ingest rules, scan databases, and flag violations with clear explanations.

Ingest & Interpret

Parse free-text PDF policy documents to extract actionable compliance rules.

Scan & Connect

Connect to live databases to identify records violating extracted rules.

Monitor & Report

Continuously monitor for new violations and provide human-in-the-loop oversight.

Detailed Requirements

Categorized breakdown of Core vs. Optional features.

Mandatory

Core Capabilities

Free-Text Ingestion

Must accept raw PDF files. The system needs to interpret natural language policies (unstructured) and convert them into structured validation logic.

Database Connection

Must connect to a live company database. The scanning mechanism must be robust enough to handle frequent data changes.

Explainable Flags

When a violation is found, the system cannot just say "Error". It must provide a clear justification linking the record to the specific policy clause.

Periodic Monitoring

One-off scans are insufficient. The agent must support scheduled or continuous monitoring to catch future violations.

Bonus
